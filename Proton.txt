Date of Discovery: 1886

Description:

A proton is a subatomic particle with
a positive electric charge of +1e 
elementary charge and mass slightly
less than that of a neutron

One or more protons are present 
in the nucleus of every atom;
they are a necessary part of 
the nucleus

The number of protons in the 
nucleus is the defining property 
of an element

Mass: 938.27MeV/c^2
